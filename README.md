# Customer Churn & Risk Analysis Dashboard

## Executive Summary
This project analyzes churn behavior across 7,043 telecommunications customers. With a churn rate of 26.54%, the analysis identifies key risk drivers impacting over $16.06M in annual revenue. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making to reduce churn and protect recurring revenue streams.


## Technical Stack
- *Power BI*: Interactive dashboards, advanced DAX measures, KPI tracking
- *Power Query*: Data cleansing, transformation, and feature engineering
- *Business Logic*: Customer risk segmentation modeling and churn indicator development


## Key Strategic Insights

- *High-Risk Services*: Fiber Optic customers exhibit a significantly higher churn rate (41.89%) compared to DSL and non-internet users.
- *Contract Risk*: Month-to-month contracts show the highest churn rate (42.71%), while long-term contracts demonstrate stronger customer retention.
- *Payment Friction*: Customers using Electronic Check have the highest churn (45.29%), indicating potential usability or trust issues.
- *Early Tenure Vulnerability*: 55.48% of churn occurs within the first year, exposing a critical onboarding and engagement gap.
- *Support Dependency*: At-risk customers generated 2,955 technical support tickets, revealing a strong correlation between service issues and churn probability.


## Data-Driven Recommendations

1. *Contract Migration Strategy*  
   Incentivize month-to-month customers to transition into annual plans through targeted retention offers.

2. *Service Quality Optimization*  
   Conduct root-cause analysis on Fiber Optic services to address technical or pricing-related churn drivers.

3. *Payment Experience Enhancement*  
   Optimize electronic check processes or promote automated payment methods to reduce friction.

4. *Early Lifecycle Retention Program*  
   Implement proactive engagement strategies within the first six months to reduce early-stage churn and maximize customer lifetime value.
